# YiCapital fixed brand standard

The YiCapital logo is immutable:

- Wordmark: `YiCapital`, with no space, on one line, in bold.
- `Yi`: pure white `#FFFFFF`.
- `Capital`: continuous left-to-right gradient `#6E9AF4` → `#B54BFA`.
- Never change capitalization, colors, spacing, arrangement, or redesign the wordmark.
- On a light background, place the unchanged logo on a sufficiently dark brand panel. Never recolor `Yi`.

This rule applies to every website page and every future visual asset: social cards, PDFs, reports, charts, email artwork, presentations, images, and exports. Product names in prose, such as "Yi Capital US", are not logo wordmarks and may retain their established names.

Canonical web markup:

```html
<span class="logo">Yi<b>Capital</b></span>
```

Canonical web colors:

```css
--brand-yi: #FFFFFF;
--brand-capital-start: #6E9AF4;
--brand-capital-end: #B54BFA;
```
