Three disciplines of cyclical mean-reversion: first, build positions only at statistical extremes of valuation and sentiment (triple-confirmed by historical percentile, implied return and crowding); second, holdings must have verifiable cash flow and clear shareholder returns — the anchor of reversion is intrinsic value, not price momentum; third, hold unlevered, manage risk with position size rather than stop-losses, and accept deep drawdowns in exchange for the full reversion path.
